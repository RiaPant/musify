ABOUT US

Share And Care is a perfect platform to widen one's horizon. Share And Care is best source of Selling, Buying and Donating Books . The portal project scope is well defined by its objectives as given above – it is primarily an Online student help Platform to allow exchange of books, donation of books to the needy and help students avail counselling on various issues from faculty and student mentors. Through the initial phase, a portal 'Construction Page' was implemented and later on a fully functional web based portal is being developed simultaneously.
